## The differentiator

`Scarce-role allocation under adversarial evidence.`

The product does not reward a move with a favorable score or recommendation. It
makes the manager surrender one outlet role, shows recorded evidence touching the
chosen priority, and then actively presents a boundary-robust shot record the
choice does not explain.

The user's felt AI/data-product strength is therefore not an avatar or chat:

1. only the selected projection reacts;
2. source records, fingerprint, and fixed context remain immutable;
3. a deterministic adversary attacks the choice;
4. missing or invalid evidence fails closed instead of producing a synthetic
   answer.

`Scout / Coach / Skeptic` are secondary implementation vocabulary. They must not
lead the first screen or the demo story.

## Five-second and 60-second contracts

The five-second memory target is exactly:

> 코너 수비에 한 명 더. 역습에는 한 명 덜.

The first instruction is exactly:

> 역습 1명을 남길지, 코너 수비 임무로 돌릴지 선택하세요.

The 60-second story order after a three-frame `0–5s` cold open is:

1. make one role move and immediately see `내 약속`;
2. play one source-linked recorded contact scene;
3. distinguish the emphasis changed by the choice from records that remain fixed;
4. switch duty once to prove a real state transition, then reset;
5. select again, open `반례 보기: 이 선택으로 설명되지 않는 슈팅 기록`,
   play it, and end on the source ID.

The emotional peak is the counterexample, not the counts, sensitivity table,
fingerprint, or reset. `42 / 14 / 17 / 10 / 1`, `10 / 42`, and the corrected
`27/81` short versus `54/81` near-side fixed-order boundary wins remain available
as trust receipts after the primary scene.

## Three fatal misreads

1. Historical 2018 evidence is read as a Brazil 2026 tendency or judged outside
   the official 2026-data scope.
2. Region contact is read as `this player would cover or stop it`, or the fixed
   outlet record is read as the counterattack caused or lost by the user's move.
3. Reactive highlighting over immutable data is read as a dashboard filter rather
   than a managerial commitment with a relinquished role and adversarial test.

## Product-gate stop rules

Do not expand Corner War Room for an unavailable fresh-user gate. The official
Data-tab rule and selected external sources now pass; those decisions do not
waive claim safety, physical accessibility, or release proof. Synthetic persona
reviews may expose defects but never become human preference or recall evidence.

After that ruling, stop or revise when any of these occur:

- transform structural audit is below `42/42`;
- independent semantic agreement is below `38/42`;
- `2/5` fresh users infer prevention, reach, result change, or caused outlet play;
- fewer than `4/5` explain the one-role tradeoff after five seconds;
- fewer than `3/5` in a separate delayed-recall cohort remember the unexplained
  shot counterexample after two hierarchy revisions; or
- the interaction is described only as filtering a chart after two revisions.
