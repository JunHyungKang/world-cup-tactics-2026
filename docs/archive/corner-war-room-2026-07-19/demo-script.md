## Recording guardrails

- Record only the frozen public URL after its build hash is known.
- The 0–5s cold open is the exact release-tree, hash-bound gallery title card. It
  is not described as a frozen-public screenshot. From 5s onward, use one
  continuous interaction take from the frozen public URL.
- Keep the pitch, selected region, and event marks visible during both replay
  windows. Return to the receipt before each explanatory/final hold; a replay
  button changing only card text is not sufficient visual proof.
- Show the 2018/result-prediction boundary on screen. Do not narrate prevention,
  reach, causality, an alternate result, optimality, xG, win probability, Brazil
  2026 tendency, AI recommendation, preference, or memorability.
- A local rehearsal or storyboard is not `youtube-public` evidence. The final
  video requires public YouTube oEmbed, owner upload confirmation, and a
  SHA-bound independent-agent artifact review with zero findings.
