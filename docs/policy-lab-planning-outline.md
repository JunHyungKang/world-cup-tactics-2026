## 1. One policy, three tournament phases

Headline: `조별리그에서 세우고, 토너먼트에서 검증하세요.` The manager
chooses two set-piece meeting priorities. The other two lanes are explicitly
excluded from priority review. The fixed campaign is 48 group-stage reference
matches, eight round-of-16 rehearsal matches, and eight sealed
quarter-final-and-later verification matches.

## 2. A real manager commitment

The manager places two priorities directly on the pitch or through accessible
cards, declares a 40%, 50%, or 60% minimum location-overlap criterion, may
abstain, and locks both choices before the match name or outcomes appear. The
score and criterion are delivery-location overlap only, never defensive
effectiveness.

## 3. Evidence argues back

Every valid corner in the revealed match appears in the ledger. A deterministic
representative contradiction is the first unselected-lane shot, otherwise the
first unselected delivery, otherwise a selected-lane shot. It is representative,
not statistically strongest. The result closes against the criterion declared
before exposure, not a threshold chosen after seeing the overlap.
